Designate a Legacy Contact

Who will reach out to Permanent to let us know of your death or permanent incapacitation? This person, your Legacy Contact, will activate your Permanent Legacy Plan. Your Legacy Contact will receive an email with instructions from Permanent, but they are not required to create an account. However, we strongly recommend that you also discuss your Legacy Plan with this person.

Permanent will send an automatic email to your Legacy Contact to inform them that they have been designated to activate your Account Legacy Plan, alongside instructions on how to do so. However, we strongly recommend that you also discuss your Legacy Plan with this person.
