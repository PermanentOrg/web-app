Manage the Legacy Plan for this Archive

Your Legacy Plan determines what should happen to your materials on Permanent.org in the event of your death or incapacitation. Your Legacy Plan has two parts:

  1. Designate an account Legacy Contact here. This person will inform Permanent of your death or incapacitation and activate your Legacy Plan as you have outlined it.
  2. Designate an Archive Steward for this archive below. An Archive Steward will assume ownership and management of this archive.
