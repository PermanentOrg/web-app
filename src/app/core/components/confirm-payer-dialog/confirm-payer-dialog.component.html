Are you sure you want to assign yourself as the payer?

  • When you become the payer of an archive, you will replace any other assigned payer.
  • All materials uploaded to this archive will count against your endowed storage balance, regardless of who uploads it.
  • Only a manager or owner of an archive can assign themself as the payer.
  • Only one payer is allowed per archive.

Are you sure you want to remove yourself as the payer?

  • When an archive does not have a designated payer, all materials uploaded to that archive will count against the uploading user’s personal storage balance.
  • Only a manager or owner of an archive can assign themself as the payer.
