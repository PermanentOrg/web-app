Create Your First Archive: Select Type

What will your first archive represent? It might be yourself, a family member, an ancestor, your neighborhood association, a small business or a nonprofit organization.

Not sure what archive option to choose? Start with yourself. You can always create new archives or change the name and type of your archive later.

What type of archive will this be? You can always change this later.

Create Your First Archive: Name Your Archive

Person Archive represents an individual person, such as yourself or someone else
Group Archive any group of people, such as a community group or a family
Organization Archive an organizational entity, such as a company or nonprofit

What is your new archive called? This is generally the legal or official name of the person, family, or organization the archive is about — for example, “The Katherine Johnson Archive.”

You can always edit your archive’s name later.
